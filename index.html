About

After years of obsession with fixing up my own homes as a side project, now I enjoy bringing my clients’ visions for their homes to life. Listening to clients and finding solutions to fit their specific needs is my top priority. I take care to communicate clearly with clients and keep them in the loop during the process so they can see their ideas coming to life. I understand that your living space is sacred and will always respect the vulnerability of you inviting me into your home. I will not hire anyone to do work with me who cannot show that same level of respect in your home.
